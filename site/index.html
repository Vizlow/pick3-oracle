Methodology

An ensemble of 19 folk Pick 3 workouts (VTRACs, rundowns, TTT grids, due pairs, followers, sum filters…) scores all 1,000 straight combos, rank-votes them Borda-style, and the vote is weighted by each tactic's exponentially-decayed trailing performance. The top 5 picks are committed to git before each draw (the lock sha is the proof), then graded against the official NY result — side-by-side with 5 random picks as a control. Backtests are labeled as backtests. Losses are shown in red, on purpose.
